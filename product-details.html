Product information

  • Product Name: High Quality Cassava Flour (HQCF)

Product Description

Our High Quality Cassava Flour (HQCF) is a versatile and nutritious product made from finely milled, fresh cassava roots. It is gluten-free, making it an excellent alternative for wheat flour in various recipes. HQCF is ideal for baking, cooking, and as a thickening agent in soups and sauces, providing a healthy and sustainable option for both households and food industries.

Product information

  • Product Name: High Quality Cassava Chips (HQCC)

Product Description

Our High Quality Cassava Chips (HQCC) are produced from carefully selected cassava roots that are sliced, dried, and processed to ensure premium quality. These chips are perfect for snacking or as an ingredient in further food processing. They offer a nutritious and energy-dense alternative to traditional potato chips, catering to health-conscious consumers seeking a wholesome snack option.

Product information

  • Product Name: Animal Feeds (Processed from Cassava Peelings)

Product Description

Our Animal Feeds, processed from cassava peelings, are a sustainable and cost-effective solution for livestock nutrition. By utilizing cassava peelings, we reduce waste and provide a high-energy feed option rich in fiber and essential nutrients. This product supports the agricultural ecosystem by offering a valuable resource for farmers to improve the health and productivity of their animals.
